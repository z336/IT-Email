Office 365

  • The latest versions of Office are 15.35.0 for Mac and v1706 for Windows.
  • Single Sign-On - We all use a lot of different apps, most of them requiring a username and password. One of the benefits of 365 is the ability to sign into 3rd party applications using your 365 account. Starting today your 365 credentials will also sign you into Concur, KnowBe4, and Udemy. Launch the application from the 365 App Picker and you will be automatically signed in. More single sign-on apps are coming soon. If you use a 3rd party app and would like to use single sign-on please contact the helpdesk.
  • Files stored in OneDrive or SharePoint can now have % and # in the filename.
  • Office Forms is in preview. Forms is a simple and lightweight app for creating polls, surveys, etc. that lets you easily gather feedback, measure satisfaction, learn what your teams think, organize team events, and more. Look for the Forms icon in your Office 365 app picker.
  • Microsoft Stream is a new enterprise video service for 365. Stream brings the power of intelligent enterprise video into a video hub, and enables knowledge sharing, communication, and connectivity in a secure enterprise environment. Look for the Stream icon in your Office 365 app picker.
  • OneDrive for Mac - Install the latest Mac version from the Mac app store or as a separate download. Before installing or deploying the client for Mac as a separate download, remove the Mac App Store version from the computer (if it was installed before).
